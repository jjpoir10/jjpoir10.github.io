SEC Network Original Writing

In March 2020, the coronavirus shut down the sports world. As a manager with the SEC Network, I had to maintain the flow of content to our website and keep my staff working with no college events to draw from.

I implemented a new content initative for original video and writing, encouraging my team to be creative and learn new skills during the height of the pandemic.

Paso Robles Press Sports Reporting

From 2013-15, I was the sports editor for the Paso Robles Press on California's Central Coast. As a young journalist, the opportunity to see a small, local newsroom in action was invaluable.

I wore many hats with the Paso Press, often working as reporter, photographer, editor, graphic designer and paginator all at once.
