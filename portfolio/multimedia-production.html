Field Reporting

Not usually one for the cameras, I decided to try my hand at field reporting for my Writing, Research & Reporting class at NYU. I produced a broadcast-style news segment reflecting on the 20th anniversary of 9/11.

The report centers on my Studio 20 colleague, Saman Shafiq, who grew up in Pakistan and has vivid memories of 9/11 as a child. She describes life in her home country as we learn about the impact of the attacks beyond our borders.
